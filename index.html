Pat's Salmon Cookie Shop

About us

Pat Cookielover, a Seattle native, has been a passionate baker for many years. Everyone loved her signature cookies that were made from salmon. First Pat's Salmon Cookie Shop was opened in Seattle in 2016, at Pike Place Market. Another four locations were open shortly after due to incredible popularity of her cookies. Treat yourself!
